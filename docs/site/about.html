About Galaxy Brain

Two undergraduates at Thapar Institute of Engineering and Technology, Patiala. We are the user we design for.

Why we built Aura

We live inside a notification flood every day. Hostel WhatsApp groups, society announcements, UPI debit alerts, food-delivery promos, IRCTC PNR statuses, lab attendance reminders. Aura is the layer we wanted on our own phones, and could not buy. We refused to ship a chatbot, refused to ship a screen-time optimiser, and refused to ship anything that needed a cloud pipe to read a UPI SMS. What is left is a small, careful orchestrator that watches what is already there, and acts a few times a day.

Constraints

  • Two-person team. No advisors, no contractors, no faculty mentor.
  • Total budget of ₹2,000. Print plus buffer. No flagship Galaxy phone in the lab.
  • Twelve-week runway across Phase 1 blueprint, Phase 2 pilot, Phase 3 demo.
  • Apple-only Phase 1 + Phase 2 reference build. Galaxy ecosystem is the production target.
